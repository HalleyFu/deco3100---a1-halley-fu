Carbon Dioxide Graph (ppm)

High Co2 levels are a sign of poorly regulated air quality. This can cause headaches, fatigue, and even stress. Workers can become more irritable which can lead to poor decision-making and increased stress levels. Using SAMBA data that tracks your workplace Aerate can help you visualise the Co2 levels in your office with our Live Dashboard.
