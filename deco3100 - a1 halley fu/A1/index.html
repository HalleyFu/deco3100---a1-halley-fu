Plotly Interaction

It's a common experience for office employees to finish work and feel tired - with only enough energy to crash, not to thrive.We easily blame this on personal choices like poor sleep or diet - factors we can control. But what if the real cause is something we can’t see. My visualisation uses the SAMBA sensor data to reveal how rising CO2, increasing temperatures, and elevated noise levels - joined together leave workers feeling fatigued before they even leave the office.
